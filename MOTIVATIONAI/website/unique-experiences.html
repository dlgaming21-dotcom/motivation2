Unique Experiences

The Invisible Italy

Moments No Guidebook Lists

Decades of relationships across Italy allow us to unlock experiences that simply cannot be booked online — private, intimate, and designed to become lifelong memories.

Gastronomy

Private Cellar Dinners

Dinner in a centuries-old wine cellar in Barolo, paired with the estate's finest vintages. Just you, the winemaker, and the silence of the Langhe hills.

Culinary Arts

Cooking in a Tuscan Villa

A morning with a local chef in a private villa kitchen — market shopping, handmade pasta, truffles. Lunch at the table where everything was made.

Culture

Sunrise Vatican Access

The Sistine Chapel before the crowds. A private art historian guides you through the Papal Apartments at a pace and depth no group tour can offer.

Nature

Truffle Hunting in Umbria

Dawn through the oak forests with a local truffle hunter and his dogs. Then back to his farmhouse kitchen to taste what you found.

Sea

Private Yacht, Amalfi Coast

A full day on the water — swimming in hidden grottos, lunch on deck, a private mooring in Positano's harbor at golden hour.

Adventure

Balloon Over Tuscany

Sunrise from above the Val d'Orcia, champagne in hand. The vineyards, the cypress trees, the hilltowns — all below you, impossibly quiet.
