About Motivation

Who We Are

Italy, Experienced From Within

Motivation is an Italian incoming luxury operator with over two decades of experience curating extraordinary travel experiences across the Italian peninsula.

We work with a select network of premium travel agencies worldwide — primarily in the United States — to deliver bespoke journeys for high net worth individuals and discerning corporate groups who demand the very best Italy has to offer.

Our team is Italian, our roots run deep, and our access to the country's finest experiences — private, exclusive, and often invisible to the public — is unmatched.
